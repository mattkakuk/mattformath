### Workaround 2: Use the "All Singular" Option

If you look at the top of your last screenshot, there is an option called
**All Singular**.

  1. Select **All Singular**.

  2. This will apply the template to _everything_ (Pages and Posts).

  3.  **If you only want it on Posts:** Click **Add Condition** , change it to **Exclude** , select **Pages** , and set it to **All**.

    *  _This is a "backdoor" way to target only posts if the direct "Posts" button is stuck._

### Workaround 3: The "Refresh & Regenerate" (If the UI is broken)

If the dropdown is completely unresponsive and won't let you select anything:

  1.  **Save** whatever you can and exit to the WordPress Dashboard.

  2. Go to **Elementor > Tools**.

  3. Click **Regenerate Files & Data**.

  4. Go to **Settings > Permalinks** and just hit **Save Changes** at the bottom (you don 't need to change anything; this just "flushes" the site's connections).

  5. Go back to your template and try the conditions again.

 **Just to be sure:** Are you using a mouse or a trackpad? Sometimes Elementor
's nested menus play better if you use the arrow keys to navigate the list and
hit **Enter** to select.

Give **Workaround 1** (typing  "All" in the last box) a shot first—it's
usually the fastest way to bypass a stuck menu.
